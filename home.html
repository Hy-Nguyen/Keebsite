Who are We?

At KeebHy, we are passionate about mechanical keyboards and would love to share our passion about this hobby with others. We aim to provide you with proper resources, such as parts, sizes, and tools so that you may be able to start putting together your first and next custom mechanical keyboard.

What Are Mechanical Keyboards?

Mechanical Keyboards are keyboards that utilizes mechanical switches to provide a more fun typing experience that comes along with amazing sound profiles. Unlike a typical office membrane keyboard, which feels mushy to the touch, mechanical switches provide a more firmer and physical press, due to the spring inside. Combined with a variety of sizes and materials for the case, or body, of the keyboard, you can get different acoustic profiles out of your keyboard.

Who Is This Hobby For?

Custom mechanical keyboards elevates the typing experience of its user. Due to its customizablitly in both aesthetics and acoustics, it welcomes individuals with all sorts of personalities and workflows.

Individuals that work with a keyboard for a majority of their work, such as programmers, business people, or even casual online chores, can all benefit from a mechanical keyboard. It can be an extension of one's personality or to match the theme, or even elevate the theme, of a workspace.
